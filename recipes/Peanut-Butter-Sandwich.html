Peanut Butter Sandwich

Home

This is a recipe for a classic, the fabled Peanut Butter Sandwich. It is really easy to make, but also amazing taste.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Sliced Bread

Steps

  1. Take 1 slice of bread, and spread Peanut Butter on it
  2. Take the another slice, and place it on the Peanut Butter you just spread, making a "sandwich"
  3. Eat and Enjoy!
